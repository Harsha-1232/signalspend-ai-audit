## FAQ

**Do I need to connect billing?**  
No. The MVP uses entered spend so a founder can run it quickly without granting account access.

**Is the audit private?**  
The public share URL removes email and company details. It shows only stack, plan, and savings numbers.

**Why show value before asking for email?**  
Founders distrust gated calculators. Showing the audit first creates trust and improves lead quality.

**What if my spend is already efficient?**  
SignalSpend says so. It captures the lead with pricing-change alerts instead of inventing weak savings.

**Where does Credex fit?**  
When savings exceed $500/month, discounted AI credits may be worth evaluating because retail-rate spend is large enough to matter.
